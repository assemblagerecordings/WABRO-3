## One-Line Identity

```text
WABRO = Electronic Music as an Open System.
```

WABRO X ASSEMBLAGE is Luke Wabro's AI-assisted creative operating system for neurofunk, experimental drum and bass, EEG/Muse 2 performance control, Bitwig project mutation, TouchDesigner/geometric visuals, snare ontology, archive scanning, open-source music, and future social-platform infrastructure.

## Creative Commons / Open Music Position

## The WABRO Snare System

The WABRO snare is a major life-work element and should be treated as both an audio object and a visual/algorithmic object.

Current conceptual layers:

- acoustic Superior Drummer-style snare routed by microphones
- synth reinforcement
- fundamental/body layer
- harmonic layer
- noise layer 1
- noise layer 2
- transient / bite
- final combined snare object

The long-term goal is to build a geometric visualiser that emulates the snare's waveform, energy structure, and component layers.

Then EEG/Muse 2 state can modulate both:

```text
snare/music parameters
visual shape parameters
```

The snare becomes the first bridge between audio ontology, visuals, and brain-state control.
